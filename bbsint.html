About Us

• Founded in 1957.

• Located in South Delhi.

• Private English Medium Co-Educational Day School.

• International Student Fraternity-13 Nationalities.

• Offers 6 Foreign Languages.

• Strength -1875 Students.

• Grade/Standard - Nursery-XII.

• Affiliation - C.B.S.E.



Our Landscape

• Bespoke teaching and learning.

• AI-powered learning technology.

• Integrated Peace Education Program.

• Citizenship & Leadership Programs

• International Ethos which provokes Glocal Thinking.

• Chaupal – the forum for Parents.
